National Award for Mitahara

Mitahara, a pioneering company dedicated to transforming dietary habits, has recently been honored with a prestigious award for its remarkable impact on over 400,000 families across the Indian states of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. This accolade recognizes Mitahara’s significant contribution to improving public health through its innovative and nutritious food offerings. Founded with the mission to promote healthier lifestyles, Mitahara has revolutionized eating habits by focusing on convenience without compromising nutritional value.

Mitahara’s salads are a vibrant celebration of fresh produce. Each salad is a colorful mix of leafy greens, crunchy vegetables, and nutrient-rich toppings, designed to deliver a satisfying crunch and a burst of flavor. The salads are not only visually appealing but also packed with antioxidants and essential nutrients, making them an excellent option for those seeking a light yet fulfilling meal.
This award celebrates Mitahara’s dedication to fostering better dietary practices and their ongoing efforts to make a positive difference in the communities they serve. As they continue to innovate and expand, Mitahara remains committed to its mission of promoting health and well-being through every meal they provide.

Our Aim to 0% Chemical Food

At Mitahara, our aim is to achieve 0% chemical food by prioritizing natural, wholesome ingredients in every product. We are committed to eliminating artificial additives, preservatives, and synthetic chemicals from our offerings. By focusing on organic and sustainable sources, we ensure that our food is not only nutritious but also free from harmful substances. Our dedication to clean, chemical-free food reflects our belief in promoting health and well-being for our consumers and the environment. We envision a future where healthy, pure food is the norm for everyone.
