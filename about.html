About Us-Our Journey


Our Story:

Mitahara is a ancient Sanskrit word derived from the vedas. mita means modern,ahara means food as we supporting the organic agriculture fosters biodiversity and reduces the pollution making it a more eco-friendly choice for the customers .This generation's focus on organic food is driven by increased awareness of health and environmental issues. Social media and information access have heightened concerns about food safety and sustainability. Additionally, there's a growing preference for transparency in food production and a desire to support practices that are perceived as more ethical and environmentally friendly.What started as one small store in telangana is now an organic product range thats available across worldwide stores, leading e-marketplaces of India and a propietary e-commerce platform - delivering healthy ingredients to doorsteps everywhere. From sourcing organic food from a single farmer, to building a community of agriculturists nation-wide, our journey has been revolutionary. We welcome you to join us for another decade of eating healthier and growing bigger!
